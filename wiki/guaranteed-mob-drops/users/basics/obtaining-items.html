Obtaining Items

You can obtain items by killing mobs, only a few mobs are able to drop pieces. Check the table below to see which mobs can drop pieces. A mob can only drop 1 of their piece, and no more.

Mob Drop Texture
Blaze Blaze Rod Piece
Skeleton Bone Marrow
Enderman Ender Pearl Piece
Creeper Gunpowder Scraps
Spider Loose String
Magma Cube Magma Cream Piece & Slime Bits
Slime Slime Bits
Wither Skeleton Withered Bone
Wither Nether Star Chunk
